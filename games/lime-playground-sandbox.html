Lime Playground Sandbox

Casual ★★★★☆
Share:

Game Description

Lime Playground Sandbox is an open-world creative sandbox game that gives you the freedom to build, experiment, and play in a physics-based environment. With a wide variety of objects, tools, and interactive elements at your disposal, you can create complex structures, design challenging obstacle courses, or simply have fun experimenting with the realistic physics engine. Whether you want to build elaborate contraptions or cause chaotic chain reactions, Lime Playground Sandbox offers endless possibilities for creativity and exploration.

Game Features:

  • Extensive library of objects, materials, and tools to build with
  • Realistic physics engine that simulates gravity, momentum, and collisions
  • Multiple environments with different properties to experiment in
  • Save and load system to preserve your creations
  • No objectives or time limits - play at your own pace

How to Play

Use your mouse to select objects from the menu and place them in the world. Left-click to interact with objects, right-click for additional options. Use the WASD keys or arrow keys to move around the environment. Experiment with different combinations of objects to see how they interact with each other. Try building structures and testing their stability, or create chain reactions using the physics system. There are no rules or objectives - let your creativity guide you and have fun exploring all the possibilities!
