About Foot Battle Ball

Foot Battle Ball is an exciting soccer game with a twist! Choose from a variety of unique characters, each with their own special abilities and stats. Compete in fast-paced matches against AI opponents or challenge your friends in local multiplayer mode. Use power-ups, special moves, and strategic gameplay to score goals and win matches!

Features:

  • Multiple playable characters with unique abilities and stats
  • Fast-paced soccer gameplay with power-ups and special moves
  • Single-player mode against AI opponents of varying difficulty
  • Local multiplayer mode for playing with friends
  • Vibrant graphics and energetic soundtrack
  • Simple controls that are easy to learn but hard to master

How to Play:

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press the spacebar or Z key to kick the ball. Time your kicks carefully to aim in the direction you want. Collect power-ups that appear on the field to gain advantages like increased speed, stronger kicks, or special abilities. Score more goals than your opponent before time runs out to win the match!
