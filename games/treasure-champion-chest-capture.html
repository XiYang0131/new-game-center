Share:

Game Description

Treasure Champion - Chest Capture is an exciting multiplayer action game where you compete against other players to collect treasures and become the ultimate champion. Navigate through challenging environments, avoid obstacles, and outmaneuver your opponents to grab as many treasure chests as possible before time runs out.

Game Features:

  • Fast-paced multiplayer gameplay with real-time competition
  • Various maps with unique layouts and challenges
  • Power-ups and special abilities to gain advantage
  • Customizable characters with different skills
  • Leaderboards to track your progress and compare with friends

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Press Space to use special abilities when available. Collect treasure chests to earn points and watch out for traps and other players trying to steal your loot. The player with the most treasure at the end of the round wins. Develop strategies to outsmart your opponents and secure your position as the Treasure Champion!
