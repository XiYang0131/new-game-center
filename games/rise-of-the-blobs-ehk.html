Rise of the Blobs EHK

Puzzle ★★★★☆
Share:

Game Description

Rise of the Blobs EHK is a colorful and addictive puzzle game where you must match and clear blobs of the same color before they rise too high. As the central column fills with colorful blobs, you'll need to rotate the playing field and strategically drop fruit of matching colors to clear them away. The game gets progressively more challenging as the speed increases, testing your reflexes and pattern recognition skills.

Game Features:

  • Vibrant, colorful visuals and engaging gameplay
  • Progressive difficulty that increases as you play
  • Special power-ups and combo mechanics
  • Multiple game modes to keep the experience fresh
  • Leaderboards to compete with friends and other players

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or swipe to rotate the central column. Match the color of the falling fruit with the blobs of the same color to clear them. Create chain reactions and combos to score more points. The game ends when the blobs reach the top of the screen, so keep clearing them efficiently to survive as long as possible. Watch out for special blobs that can help or hinder your progress!
