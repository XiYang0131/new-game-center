About Space.io VFT

Space.io VFT is an exciting multiplayer space battle game where you pilot a spacecraft and compete against players from around the world. Navigate through the vast cosmos, collect resources to upgrade your ship, and engage in intense dogfights with other players. With various ship types to unlock, weapons to master, and strategies to employ, this game offers endless hours of space combat action!

Features:

  • Real-time multiplayer battles with players worldwide
  • Multiple spacecraft types with unique abilities and stats
  • Upgrade system to enhance your ship's weapons, speed, and defense
  • Resource collection and management
  • Beautiful space environments with stunning visual effects
  • Leaderboard system to track your ranking
  • Fast-paced, action-packed gameplay

How to Play:

Use your mouse to control your spacecraft's movement and aim. Left-click to fire your primary weapon. Collect glowing resources scattered throughout space to earn points and upgrade your ship. Avoid enemy fire and use asteroids as cover during battles. Destroy enemy ships to climb the leaderboard. Watch your health bar and retreat when necessary to recover. Upgrade your ship between respawns to gain advantages in battle. Experiment with different ship types to find the one that matches your playstyle. Team up with other players for cooperative missions or engage in free-for-all combat.
