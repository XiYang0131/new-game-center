About Ragdoll Break

Ragdoll Break is a hilarious physics-based arcade game where you launch ragdoll characters into various obstacles and watch the chaos unfold. Your goal is to cause as much destruction as possible by strategically aiming your ragdolls to break objects, trigger chain reactions, and rack up points. With realistic physics, unpredictable outcomes, and increasingly challenging levels, this game offers endless entertainment!

Features

  • Realistic physics engine for authentic ragdoll movements
  • Multiple levels with different obstacles and challenges
  • Various ragdoll characters with unique properties
  • Special power-ups to enhance your destruction capabilities
  • Score multipliers for impressive chain reactions
  • Hilarious animations and sound effects

How to Play

Click and drag to aim your ragdoll launcher. Release to fire the ragdoll. The longer you hold, the more power you'll put into your shot. Try to hit objects marked with point values to earn score. Some objects can trigger chain reactions, so plan your shots carefully to maximize destruction. You have a limited number of ragdolls per level, so use them wisely. Complete level objectives to unlock new stages and ragdoll characters with special abilities!
