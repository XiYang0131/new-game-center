Dogs vs Aliens

Strategy ★★★★☆
Share:

Game Description

Dogs vs Aliens is a charming and entertaining tower defense game where you command a team of heroic canines to protect Earth from an alien invasion. Deploy different dog breeds with unique abilities along the aliens' path to stop them from reaching their goal. Each dog type has special skills - from long-range attackers to area-of-effect defenders - allowing for diverse strategic approaches. Upgrade your furry defenders, unlock new dog breeds, and use special abilities to repel the increasingly challenging waves of extraterrestrial invaders.

Game Features:

  • Multiple dog breeds with unique abilities and attack styles
  • Various alien types with different strengths and weaknesses
  • Upgrade system to enhance your canine defenders
  • Special abilities and power-ups to turn the tide of battle
  • Progressively challenging levels with diverse environments

How to Play

Click on available spots along the path to place your dog defenders. Each dog costs a certain amount of resources, which regenerate over time. Choose dog types strategically based on the incoming alien waves - some dogs are better against specific alien types. Upgrade your dogs by clicking on them and selecting the upgrade option. Use special abilities when available by clicking on their icons at the bottom of the screen. Plan your defense carefully and adapt to changing alien patterns to successfully defend Earth from the invasion!

Player Comments

DogLover123 2024-03-05

Dogs vs Aliens combines two of my favorite things - dogs and tower defense games! The different dog breeds are adorable and each has useful abilities that make strategic planning fun. I love the Golden Retriever's area attack and the German Shepherd's high damage output. The game gets pretty challenging in later levels, but upgrading your dogs makes a huge difference. The sound effects of dogs barking at aliens always makes me laugh. Definitely recommend this to anyone who enjoys tower defense games with a cute twist!

StrategyMaster 2024-03-03

This game has a perfect balance of challenge and fun. The concept is silly but the gameplay is surprisingly deep. I've found that mixing fast-attacking dogs with heavy damage dealers works best for most levels. Don't underestimate the Chihuahua - its speed boost ability can save you in tight situations! The resource management aspect adds another layer of strategy since you need to decide whether to place more dogs or upgrade existing ones. My only suggestion would be to add more levels, as I finished all of them and want more!
