About Zombie Crusher OOZ

Zombie Crusher OOZ is an action-packed driving game where you take control of powerful vehicles to crush hordes of zombies. Navigate through apocalyptic environments, upgrade your vehicles, and eliminate as many undead as possible. With multiple vehicles to unlock, various zombie types to crush, and challenging levels to complete, this game offers hours of zombie-smashing fun!

Features:

  • Multiple powerful vehicles to unlock and upgrade
  • Various zombie types with different behaviors and strengths
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Upgrade system to enhance your vehicle's performance
  • Realistic physics and satisfying crushing mechanics
  • Post-apocalyptic environments with detailed graphics
  • Action-packed gameplay with intense zombie encounters

How to Play:

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Drive over zombies to crush them and earn points. Collect power-ups and coins scattered throughout the levels to upgrade your vehicle. Complete level objectives to progress and unlock new vehicles. Watch out for special zombies that may be more difficult to crush or have unique abilities. Manage your vehicle's health as collisions with obstacles can damage it. Upgrade your vehicle between levels to improve its speed, durability, and crushing power.
