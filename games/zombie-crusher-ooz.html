About Zombie Crusher OOZ

Zombie Crusher OOZ is an action-packed survival game where you take control of powerful vehicles to crush hordes of the undead. In a world overrun by zombies, your mission is to clear areas of the infected using modified vehicles equipped with deadly weapons and reinforced frames. Drive through waves of zombies, collect resources, upgrade your vehicles, and survive as long as possible in this adrenaline-fueled zombie apocalypse game. With multiple vehicles to unlock, various zombie types to crush, and challenging environments to navigate, Zombie Crusher OOZ offers hours of intense zombie-smashing action.

Features

  • Multiple vehicles with unique handling and crushing capabilities
  • Extensive upgrade system for speed, armor, and weapons
  • Various zombie types with different behaviors and strengths
  • Multiple environments and mission types
  • Resource collection and management
  • Challenging boss zombies
  • Satisfying physics-based zombie crushing mechanics
  • Progression system with unlockable content

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Up/W accelerates, Down/S brakes and reverses, Left/A and Right/D steer. Press Space to activate special abilities or weapons when they're charged. Drive over zombies to crush them and earn points. Different vehicles have different weights and crushing capabilities - heavier vehicles can crush more zombies but are slower and harder to maneuver. Collect resources dropped by zombies to earn currency for upgrades. Between levels, visit the garage to upgrade your vehicle's speed, armor, fuel capacity, and weapons. Some zombies require specific tactics to defeat - watch out for exploding zombies that can damage your vehicle, and armored zombies that are resistant to being crushed. Complete mission objectives to progress through the game and unlock new areas and vehicles. Keep an eye on your vehicle's health and fuel - if either runs out, it's game over. Use nitro boosts (activated by pressing Shift) strategically to escape from large hordes or to quickly reach objectives.

Comments

ZombieSlayer99 2023-10-20

This game is incredibly satisfying! There's something therapeutic about plowing through hordes of zombies with a souped-up monster truck. The physics are really well done - zombies go flying in all directions when you hit them at high speed. I've unlocked most of the vehicles, and the tank is definitely my favorite for pure crushing power, though the sports car is great when you need to complete timed missions. Pro tip: Focus on armor upgrades first, then weapons. You can always drive around to avoid zombies, but when you get surrounded, you need to be able to take some hits while you fight your way out.

ApocalypseSurvivor 2023-10-15

The variety of missions keeps this game interesting even after hours of play. I particularly enjoy the escort missions where you have to protect a civilian vehicle - they add a nice strategic element since you can't just focus on crushing zombies. The boss zombies are genuinely challenging too, especially the Behemoth that appears in the later levels. It took me several attempts to figure out its pattern and weak spots. The upgrade system is deep enough to be engaging without being overwhelming. My only suggestion would be to add more environmental hazards or interactive elements to the maps to add even more variety.

CasualGamer42 2023-10-10

This is the perfect game to play when you just want to blow off some steam. The controls are simple enough that you can jump right in, but there's enough depth to keep you coming back. I love the sound design - the squishing noises when you run over zombies and the engine roars are so satisfying. The night missions add an extra layer of challenge since you can only see what's in your headlights. For beginners, I recommend starting with the pickup truck - it has a good balance of speed and crushing power. Also, don't forget to use your nitro boost to escape when you get surrounded! Looking forward to seeing what new vehicles they might add in updates.
