About Knight Clicker

Knight Clicker is an addictive idle/clicker game where you play as a brave knight on a quest to defeat monsters and become the most powerful hero in the realm. Click to attack monsters, earn gold, and upgrade your equipment and abilities. The more you upgrade, the more damage you deal and the faster you progress through the game.

Features

  • Simple yet addictive clicking gameplay
  • Upgrade your knight's weapons, armor, and abilities
  • Battle various monsters with increasing difficulty
  • Earn achievements and unlock special bonuses
  • Idle progression - earn gold even when not actively playing
  • Prestige system for long-term progression

How to Play

Click on the monster to attack and deal damage. As you defeat monsters, you'll earn gold that can be spent on upgrades. Purchase weapon upgrades to increase your click damage, armor upgrades to improve your defense, and ability upgrades for special effects. Hire companions to automatically deal damage over time, allowing you to progress even when not actively clicking. Prestige when you reach a certain point to earn special currencies and bonuses for your next run!
