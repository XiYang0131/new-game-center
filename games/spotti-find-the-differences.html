About Spotti - Find the Differences

Spotti - Find the Differences is a challenging observation game that tests your ability to spot subtle differences between two seemingly identical images. Each level presents you with a pair of images that look almost the same, but contain several differences that you need to find. Sharpen your eyes and attention to detail as you progress through increasingly difficult levels!

Features

  • Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
  • Beautiful, detailed images to examine
  • Hint system to help when you're stuck
  • Time-based challenges to test your speed
  • Track your progress and improve your observation skills
  • Suitable for all ages - a great brain exercise!

How to Play

Look carefully at both images and click on the differences you spot. Each level has a specific number of differences to find. If you're having trouble, you can use hints to reveal a difference, but hints are limited! Try to find all differences within the time limit to earn bonus points. The faster you complete each level, the higher your score will be. Challenge yourself to improve your observation skills and beat your previous times!
