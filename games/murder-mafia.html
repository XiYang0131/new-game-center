Game Description

Murder Mafia is an intriguing detective game that puts your investigative skills to the test. Step into the shoes of a skilled detective tasked with solving complex murder cases in a city plagued by mafia activity. Explore crime scenes, collect evidence, interrogate suspects, and piece together clues to uncover the truth behind each mysterious death. With multiple cases to solve, each with its own unique storyline and suspects, Murder Mafia offers hours of engaging gameplay for mystery enthusiasts.

Game Features:

  • Multiple murder cases with unique storylines and characters
  • Detailed crime scenes with hidden clues and evidence
  • Interrogation system to question suspects and witnesses
  • Evidence analysis tools to connect clues and build your case
  • Challenging puzzles that test your deductive reasoning

How to Play

Use your mouse to navigate through different locations and interact with objects and characters. Click on items of interest to collect evidence and add them to your inventory. Examine collected evidence by clicking on it in your inventory. During interrogations, choose your questions carefully as they can influence how suspects respond. Connect related pieces of evidence in your case file to form conclusions. Once you believe you've identified the culprit, present your case with supporting evidence. Be thorough in your investigation - overlooking even a small detail could lead you to the wrong conclusion!
