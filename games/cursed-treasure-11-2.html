Cursed Treasure 11-2

Strategy ★★★★★
Share:

Game Description

Cursed Treasure 11-2 is a captivating tower defense game where you play as an evil overlord protecting your precious gems from waves of heroic adventurers. Build and upgrade three types of defensive towers - Orc Dens, Demon Spires, and Undead Crypts - each with unique abilities and attack methods. Strategically place your towers to create the perfect defense system, unlock powerful spells, and upgrade your evil powers through an extensive skill tree. With challenging levels, diverse enemy types, and a humorous evil twist on the classic tower defense formula, Cursed Treasure 11-2 offers hours of strategic gameplay.

Game Features:

  • Three unique tower types with different attack methods and special abilities
  • Extensive skill tree with upgradeable powers and abilities
  • Multiple enemy types with different strengths, weaknesses, and movement patterns
  • Special spells and abilities to turn the tide of battle
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty and strategic depth

How to Play

Click on available building spots to construct towers. Each tower type can only be built on specific terrain: Orc Dens on forests, Demon Spires on mountains, and Undead Crypts on graveyards. Upgrade your towers by clicking on them and selecting the upgrade option. Use mana collected from defeated enemies to cast spells and activate special abilities. Between levels, spend skill points to enhance your towers and unlock new abilities in the skill tree. Plan your defense carefully - different enemies have different resistances and movement speeds. Cover all possible paths to your gems and focus on creating bottlenecks where your towers can concentrate fire. Remember that flying enemies ignore the path and head straight for your treasures, so prepare air defenses!

Player Comments

EvilOverlord 2024-04-02

Cursed Treasure 11-2 is one of the best tower defense games I've ever played! The balance between the three tower types is perfect - Orc Dens for slowing enemies, Demon Spires for area damage, and Undead Crypts for high single-target damage. I love how the skill tree lets you customize your playstyle - I focused on maxing out the Undead path first for those powerful skull shots. The difficulty curve is well-designed, gradually introducing new enemy types that force you to adapt your strategy. Level 15 was particularly challenging with those pesky flying paladins! For new players, I recommend prioritizing the "Greed" skill early on to maximize your mana income. Also, don't neglect your spells - the Meteor shower can save you in tight situations!

TowerDefenseGuru 2024-03-31

This game has the perfect blend of strategy and humor! I love the evil perspective - it's refreshing to play as the "bad guy" for once. The game mechanics are solid, with meaningful choices in both tower placement and skill tree development. My strategy revolves around creating choke points with upgraded Orc Dens to slow enemies, then placing Demon Spires nearby for maximum damage over time. The boss waves require special attention - I always save my mana for spells during these waves. One tip for getting perfect scores: don't build too many towers initially. Start with just enough defense, then save up mana for upgrades rather than building new towers everywhere. The replay value is excellent too, as different skill builds can completely change your approach to each level. Definitely a must-play for tower defense fans!
