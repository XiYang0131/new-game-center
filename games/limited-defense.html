About Limited Defense

Limited Defense is a challenging tower defense game with a unique twist - you have a limited number of towers to place! Unlike traditional tower defense games where you can build as many towers as your resources allow, in Limited Defense you must carefully consider each tower placement as you have a fixed number for each level. This limitation forces you to think strategically about tower positioning, upgrades, and synergies to maximize your defensive capabilities.

Features

  • Unique limited tower placement mechanic
  • Multiple tower types with different abilities and attack patterns
  • Strategic upgrade system to enhance your towers
  • Diverse enemy types requiring different defensive strategies
  • Challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Beautiful graphics and satisfying visual effects
  • Engaging soundtrack that enhances the gameplay experience

How to Play

Click on an empty spot on the map to place a tower. Each level gives you a specific number of towers you can place. Choose tower types wisely based on the enemies you'll face. Upgrade your towers by clicking on them and selecting the upgrade option. Some towers are better against fast enemies, while others excel against armored foes. Watch the enemy path carefully and place towers at strategic chokepoints for maximum effectiveness. If too many enemies reach your base, you'll lose the level. Complete all waves to progress to the next challenge!
