Game Description

Miniblox is a creative sandbox game that lets you build and explore in a colorful block-based world. Express your creativity by placing blocks, creating structures, and designing your own unique environments. With simple controls and endless possibilities, Miniblox offers a relaxing and engaging experience for players of all ages.

Game Features:

  • Open sandbox environment with unlimited building potential
  • Various block types with different colors and properties
  • Simple and intuitive building mechanics
  • Save and load your creations
  • Suitable for players of all ages and skill levels

How to Play

Use your mouse to place and remove blocks. Left-click to place a block, right-click to remove. Select different block types from the menu at the bottom of the screen. Move around the world using WASD keys or arrow keys. Let your imagination run wild and create anything from simple houses to complex structures!
