Murder

Puzzle ★★★★★
Share:

Game Description

Murder is an engaging detective puzzle game where players take on the role of a detective, investigating various murder cases, collecting evidence, analyzing clues, and ultimately identifying the culprit. The game offers multiple different crime scenes, each with its own unique storyline and challenges.

Game Features:

  • Multiple carefully designed murder case scenarios
  • Engaging storylines with interesting characters
  • Need to logically reconstruct the sequence of events
  • Detailed graphics and atmospheric sound effects
  • Intuitive point-and-click interface

Challenge your detective skills, see if you can solve all the mysteries, and find the true culprits!

Game Guide

Use your mouse to interact with objects and areas in the crime scene, collect evidence, and talk to suspects. Pay attention to details, as important clues might be hidden in unexpected places.

Controls:

  • Mouse Click - Interact with objects and characters
  • Inventory - Store collected evidence and clues
  • Notebook - Record important information and evidence
  • Map - Navigate between different locations

Remember to thoroughly examine each scene and pay attention to small details, as they could be crucial to solving the case.

Comments

MysteryFan 2024-01-25

As a mystery novel enthusiast, I absolutely love this game! Each case is cleverly designed with well-placed clues that are neither too obvious nor too obscure. I particularly enjoyed the timeline reconstruction mechanism in the third case, which made the investigation process more dynamic. The atmosphere is excellently crafted, with sound effects and visuals that enhance the tension. If you enjoy stories like Sherlock Holmes or Murder on the Orient Express, you'll definitely love this game!

DetectiveGamer 2024-01-22

This is one of the best detective games I've played! The puzzles are challenging but not frustrating. I love the deduction system that makes you feel like a real detective analyzing evidence. The character development is excellent too, with each suspect having their own motives and personalities. For those stuck on the fourth case, I recommend checking the kitchen trash can carefully - there's a crucial clue there that's easy to miss. Looking forward to more cases from the developers!
