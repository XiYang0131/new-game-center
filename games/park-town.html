About Park Town

Welcome to Park Town, an engaging park management simulation game! In this game, you become the manager of a city park, responsible for designing, building, and operating an amazing entertainment venue. Starting with simple benches and flower beds, gradually expand to exciting rides, restaurants, and various attractions to attract more visitors and create a thriving park kingdom.

Features

  • Freely create and design your ideal park
  • Unlock various rides, decorations, and buildings
  • Manage park finances, balance income and expenses
  • Meet visitor needs and improve satisfaction
  • Deal with various challenges and emergencies
  • Complete missions for additional rewards
  • Unlock new areas as your park develops

How to Play

Use your mouse to click on buttons in the interface to build and manage your park. Left-click to select items and drag to place facilities. Right-click to cancel selection or delete placed items. Pay attention to visitor needs and feedback to continuously improve your park. Plan roads and facility layouts carefully to ensure visitors can easily reach all attractions. As the game progresses, you'll unlock more advanced facilities and features to make your park even more exciting!
