Gridle

Puzzle ★★★★☆
Share:

Game Description

Gridle is an engaging word puzzle game inspired by the popular Wordle format but with unique twists that add fresh challenges. In this grid-based word-guessing game, you'll test your vocabulary and deduction skills as you attempt to identify the hidden word within a limited number of tries. Each guess provides color-coded feedback that helps narrow down the correct letters and their positions. With its clean interface, intuitive gameplay, and daily challenges, Gridle offers a perfect brain exercise that's easy to learn but satisfyingly difficult to master. Challenge yourself to solve each puzzle in as few attempts as possible and compare your results with friends!

Game Features:

  • Grid-based word puzzle gameplay with intuitive feedback system
  • Daily challenges with new words to discover
  • Multiple difficulty levels to suit different skill levels
  • Statistics tracking to monitor your progress and improvement
  • Clean, minimalist design that focuses on the puzzle experience

How to Play

The goal of Gridle is to guess the hidden word within six attempts. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. After submitting a guess, the letters will change color to provide feedback: green indicates a correct letter in the correct position, yellow shows a correct letter in the wrong position, and gray means the letter is not in the target word at all. Use this feedback to refine your subsequent guesses and narrow down the possibilities. A good strategy is to start with words that contain common letters like E, A, R, T, and S to quickly identify which letters are in the target word. Pay attention to both the presence of letters and their positions - sometimes rearranging the same letters can lead to the solution. The game includes an on-screen keyboard that also changes color to help you keep track of which letters you've tried and their results. Try to solve the puzzle in as few attempts as possible! The game tracks your statistics, including your success rate, current streak, and average number of guesses, allowing you to monitor your improvement over time. New puzzles are available daily, so you can make Gridle part of your regular brain-training routine!
