About Enemania

Enemania is an intense action shooter game where you face waves of increasingly difficult enemies. Take control of a powerful warrior and battle through hordes of monsters using a variety of weapons and special abilities. Upgrade your character, unlock new weapons, and test your skills against challenging boss fights. With fast-paced gameplay and non-stop action, Enemania will keep you on the edge of your seat!

Features

  • Intense action gameplay with waves of enemies
  • Multiple weapons and power-ups to collect
  • Character upgrade system with skill trees
  • Epic boss battles that test your skills
  • Dynamic environments with destructible elements
  • Special abilities and combo attacks
  • Challenging achievements to unlock

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Left-click to fire your weapon and right-click to use special abilities. Press Space to dodge incoming attacks. Collect power-ups that appear on the battlefield to gain temporary advantages like increased damage, speed, or invincibility. Defeat enemies to earn experience points and gold, which can be used to upgrade your character's abilities and purchase new weapons. Watch out for boss enemies that appear after certain waves - they have unique attack patterns and require strategy to defeat. Remember to keep moving to avoid enemy attacks and make use of the environment for cover when needed.
