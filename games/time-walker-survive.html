About Time Walker Survive

Time Walker Survive is an exciting endless runner game where you play as a time traveler navigating through different time periods. Your mission is to survive as long as possible while dodging obstacles, collecting power-ups, and experiencing the changing landscapes of history and the future. With increasing speed and difficulty as you progress, this game will test your reflexes and timing to the limit!

Features

  • Fast-paced endless runner gameplay with increasing difficulty
  • Multiple time periods with unique obstacles and environments
  • Various power-ups to enhance your abilities and survival chances
  • Time-shifting mechanics that change the gameplay dynamics
  • Unlockable characters with different abilities
  • Global leaderboards to compete with players worldwide
  • Stunning visuals and immersive sound effects

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press UP or W to jump, and DOWN or S to slide under obstacles. Collect time crystals (glowing blue objects) to fill your time-shift meter. Once full, press SPACE to activate a time-shift, which temporarily slows down obstacles and makes them easier to avoid. Look out for power-ups like shields (protects from one hit), magnets (attracts nearby collectibles), and time freezers (temporarily stops all obstacles). The game gets progressively faster and more challenging the longer you survive, so stay alert and time your movements carefully!
