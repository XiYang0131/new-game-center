About Idle Saga

Idle Saga is an epic idle RPG adventure where you recruit heroes, battle monsters, and collect treasures in a fantasy world. The game continues to progress even when you're not actively playing, allowing your party to grow stronger, collect resources, and advance through the story. With a wide variety of heroes to recruit, equipment to collect, and skills to master, Idle Saga offers endless progression and strategic depth.

Features

  • Recruit and upgrade a diverse roster of heroes with unique abilities
  • Automatic combat system with strategic hero positioning
  • Collect and enhance powerful equipment for your heroes
  • Progress through an epic campaign with challenging boss battles
  • Offline progression - earn rewards even when you're not playing
  • Multiple game modes including campaign, tower challenges, and PvP arena
  • Regular events and updates with new content

How to Play

Start by recruiting your first heroes and sending them on adventures. Heroes will automatically fight enemies and collect resources. Use these resources to upgrade your heroes' levels, skills, and equipment. Position your heroes strategically to maximize their effectiveness in battle - tanks in front to absorb damage, damage dealers in the middle, and support heroes in the back. Complete daily quests for additional rewards and participate in special events. Remember to check in regularly to collect offline rewards and make strategic decisions about hero upgrades and team composition. The key to success is finding the right balance between different hero types and optimizing your resource allocation!
