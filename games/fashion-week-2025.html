About Fashion Week 2025

Fashion Week 2025 is a futuristic fashion design game that lets you create cutting-edge outfits for the runway of tomorrow. Set in the year 2025, this game combines fashion design with futuristic elements like holographic fabrics, smart textiles, and augmented reality accessories. Express your creativity and become a renowned fashion designer in this stylish and innovative game.

Features

  • Design futuristic fashion outfits
  • Hundreds of clothing items and accessories
  • Unique futuristic materials and styles
  • Runway shows to showcase your creations
  • Compete against AI designers

How to Play

Select a model and start designing their outfit by choosing from various clothing items, accessories, and materials. Mix and match different elements to create a cohesive and stylish look. Once you're satisfied with your design, send your model to the runway and see how your creation is received by the judges. Earn points and unlock new items based on your performance!
