About War Groups

War Groups is an engaging strategy game where you command troops in intense battlefield scenarios. Deploy different unit types, manage resources, and outmaneuver your opponents with tactical precision. With multiple levels, diverse units, and challenging enemies, this game offers a deep strategic experience for military enthusiasts.

Features:

  • Multiple campaign levels with increasing difficulty
  • Various unit types with unique abilities and strengths
  • Resource management and strategic deployment
  • Tactical combat with positioning and timing elements
  • Upgrade system to enhance your troops
  • Challenging AI opponents with different tactics
  • Engaging battlefield visuals and sound effects

How to Play:

Click on your units to select them, then click on the battlefield to move or attack. Manage your resources carefully to deploy new units. Different units have different strengths and weaknesses - infantry are versatile, tanks are powerful but slow, and aircraft provide mobility. Position your units strategically to take advantage of terrain and outflank your enemies. Complete mission objectives to progress through the campaign. Upgrade your units between missions to improve their effectiveness in battle.
