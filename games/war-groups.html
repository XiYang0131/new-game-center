About War Groups

War Groups is a strategic multiplayer battle game that challenges your tactical thinking and resource management skills. Command your troops across various battlefields, build your base, gather resources, and defeat enemy forces. With a variety of units, buildings, and technologies to research, War Groups offers deep strategic gameplay that rewards careful planning and quick decision-making. Compete against other players or AI opponents in this engaging military strategy game.

Features

  • Real-time strategic combat with multiple unit types
  • Base building and resource management
  • Technology tree with various upgrades and abilities
  • Multiple factions with unique units and playstyles
  • PvP and PvE game modes
  • Ranked matchmaking system
  • Customizable army compositions
  • Challenging campaign missions

How to Play

Use your mouse to select units and issue commands. Right-click to move units or attack enemy forces. Build resource collectors to gather materials needed for constructing buildings and training troops. Establish a strong economy before focusing on military production. Scout the map to locate enemy bases and resources. Adapt your strategy based on your opponent's army composition and base layout. Research technologies to unlock more powerful units and abilities. Protect your resource collectors and main base while looking for opportunities to attack enemy weak points. Form alliances with other players in team modes for coordinated attacks. Remember that a balanced army with different unit types is usually more effective than focusing on a single unit type.
