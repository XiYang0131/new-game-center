Cursed Treasure Level Pack

Strategy ★★★★★
Share:

Game Description

Cursed Treasure Level Pack is an expansion to the popular tower defense game that brings new challenges and levels to test your strategic skills. Continue your role as an evil overlord protecting precious gems from waves of heroic adventurers. This level pack introduces new map layouts, enemy types, and strategic challenges while maintaining the core gameplay that made the original so addictive. Build and upgrade your three tower types - Orc Dens, Demon Spires, and Undead Crypts - each with unique abilities, and use your evil powers to repel the forces of good. With your skill tree progression carrying over, you can continue to develop your evil empire and perfect your defensive strategies.

Game Features:

  • New challenging levels with unique layouts and strategic opportunities
  • Three tower types with different strengths and special abilities
  • Continued skill tree progression from the original game
  • Additional enemy types with new movement patterns and abilities
  • Special spells and powers to enhance your defensive capabilities

How to Play

Click on available terrain to build towers: Orc Dens on forests, Demon Spires on mountains, and Undead Crypts on graveyards. Each tower type has different strengths - Orcs slow enemies, Demons deal area damage, and Undead provide high single-target damage. Upgrade your towers by clicking on them and selecting the upgrade option. Collect mana from defeated enemies to cast spells and activate special abilities. Between levels, spend skill points to enhance your towers and unlock new abilities in the skill tree. Plan your defense carefully to cover all possible paths to your gems. Create bottlenecks where your towers can focus fire on enemies. Remember that flying enemies will ignore the path and head straight for your treasures, so prepare appropriate defenses. Aim for perfect scores by preventing any gems from being stolen!

GemDefender 2024-04-05

This level pack is exactly what I needed after finishing the original game! The new maps provide fresh challenges that require rethinking strategies that worked before. I particularly enjoy the third level with its multiple entry points - it really tests your ability to manage resources and cover all vulnerable areas. The difficulty progression is well-balanced, starting with manageable levels before ramping up to truly challenging scenarios. My favorite strategy is to focus on maxing out the Undead skill tree first for those powerful skull shots, then investing in the Demon path for area control. For those struggling with the later levels, don't forget about your spells - the freeze spell combined with meteor shower can save you when things get overwhelming!

StrategyMaster 2024-04-03

The Cursed Treasure Level Pack maintains everything I loved about the original while adding new challenges that kept me engaged for hours. The new enemy types force you to adapt your strategies - those teleporting mages in particular require special attention! I found that a balanced approach to the skill tree works best for these levels, as you need versatility to handle the diverse threats. One tip that helped me achieve perfect scores: don't rush to build towers everywhere at the start. Instead, focus on key chokepoints and gradually expand your defenses as you accumulate resources. The humor in this game continues to be a highlight - I love the evil overlord perspective and the amusing descriptions of the heroes. If you enjoyed the original Cursed Treasure, this level pack is an absolute must-play!
