About Kingdom Solitaire

Kingdom Solitaire is a beautiful fantasy-themed solitaire card game with stunning visuals and addictive gameplay. Immerse yourself in a magical world while enjoying classic solitaire mechanics with unique twists.

Features

  • Beautiful fantasy-themed artwork
  • Classic solitaire gameplay with unique twists
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Relaxing soundtrack
  • Daily challenges and achievements

How to Play

Arrange cards in descending order, alternating between red and black suits. Build foundation piles in ascending order by suit. Clear all cards from the tableau to win!
