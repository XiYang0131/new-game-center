About Pokey Ball

Pokey Ball is an exciting arcade game that challenges your reflexes and timing. Control a bouncy ball as you navigate through increasingly difficult obstacle courses. Your goal is to reach the finish line without falling off the path or hitting hazards. With simple controls but challenging gameplay, Pokey Ball offers a perfect balance of fun and difficulty that will keep you coming back for more.

Features

  • Dozens of challenging levels with increasing difficulty
  • Smooth and responsive ball physics
  • Various obstacles and hazards to overcome
  • Colorful and vibrant 3D graphics
  • Simple one-touch controls
  • Collect stars to unlock new ball skins
  • Compete with friends for the best completion times

How to Play

Tap or click to make the ball jump. Hold longer for higher jumps. Time your jumps carefully to avoid obstacles and stay on the path. Collect stars along the way to earn points and unlock new ball skins. The path becomes narrower and more challenging as you progress through levels. Some platforms move or disappear, requiring precise timing. If you fall off the path, you'll need to restart the level. Try to complete each level as quickly as possible to earn a higher score. Remember that patience and timing are key to mastering this game!
