About Girls Nail Salon

Girls Nail Salon is a fashion nail design game where you can create and decorate beautiful nails in various styles. Choose from different colors, patterns, stickers, and accessories to create unique nail art designs. Show off your creativity and fashion sense as a nail designer!

Features

  • Wide variety of nail shapes and lengths
  • Multiple colors and textures to choose from
  • Various stickers, gems, and decorations
  • Unlimited creative design possibilities
  • Save and share your creations

How to Play

Select a model and start designing her nails. Use various tools to trim, shape, and color the nails. Add patterns, stickers, and decorations to perfect your design. When you're done, you can save your work and move on to the next challenge.
