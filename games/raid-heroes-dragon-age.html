Raid Heroes: Dragon Age

RPG ★★★★★
Share:

Game Description

Raid Heroes: Dragon Age is an immersive turn-based RPG that transports you to a fantasy realm where dragons have returned to terrorize the land. As the commander of an elite band of heroes, you must strategically build your team, develop their unique abilities, and lead them through challenging quests and epic battles. The game features a rich narrative with diverse characters, each with their own backstories and special skills. From mighty warriors and cunning rogues to powerful mages and devoted healers, you'll need to carefully select and position your heroes to overcome increasingly difficult enemies. With its deep tactical combat system, extensive character progression, and captivating dragon-hunting storyline, Raid Heroes: Dragon Age offers an engaging RPG experience that will challenge your strategic thinking and reward your perseverance.

Game Features:

  • Strategic turn-based combat with positioning and ability synergies
  • Over 30 unique heroes to collect, each with distinct abilities and roles
  • Extensive gear system with set bonuses and powerful enchantments
  • Epic boss battles against various dragon types with unique mechanics
  • Engaging storyline with multiple quest branches and challenging dungeons

How to Play

Begin your adventure by selecting your starting heroes from different classes - Warriors provide frontline protection, Mages deal area damage, Rogues excel at single-target damage, and Healers keep your team alive. As you progress through the tutorial, you'll learn the basics of combat, which takes place on a grid-based battlefield. During combat, heroes take turns based on their speed stat. When it's a hero's turn, you can move them on the grid and use one of their abilities. Position is crucial - some abilities affect specific areas, and heroes receive bonuses for attacking from certain positions (like rogues dealing more damage from behind enemies). Each hero has unique abilities that consume mana, as well as a basic attack that doesn't require mana. Heroes gain experience from battles, leveling up to increase their stats and unlock new abilities. Collect gear from defeated enemies and completed quests to equip your heroes, enhancing their stats and sometimes providing special effects. As you progress, you'll unlock the ability to upgrade gear, enhance abilities, and even evolve your heroes to more powerful forms. The main campaign follows your quest to defeat increasingly powerful dragons, with side quests offering additional rewards and story content. For challenging content, form a balanced team that includes tanks to absorb damage, damage dealers to eliminate enemies, and support characters to heal and buff your team. Remember that different enemies have various resistances and vulnerabilities - adapt your strategy accordingly. The game also features a guild system where you can join other players, participate in guild raids, and earn exclusive rewards.

Player Comments

DragonSlayer88 2024-05-30

Raid Heroes: Dragon Age has completely exceeded my expectations for a browser-based RPG! The depth of the combat system is impressive - positioning your heroes correctly and timing your abilities makes a huge difference in difficult battles. I've been playing for about three weeks now and have collected 18 different heroes. My current favorite team composition is Valorn (tank), Lyra (healer), Thorne (archer), and Ignis (fire mage). This setup gives me good survivability while still having enough damage output to handle most encounters. The Crimson Drake boss on level 24 was particularly challenging until I realized you need to focus on taking out his healing crystals first. For new players, I highly recommend prioritizing the "Hero Academy" upgrade early on, as it significantly increases your heroes' experience gain. Also, save your premium currency for the guaranteed legendary hero summons rather than spending it on random pulls. The storyline has some genuinely interesting twists, especially when you reach the ancient temple area. Can't wait to see what new content they add in future updates!

TacticalGamer 2024-05-27

As someone who loves tactical RPGs, I found Raid Heroes: Dragon Age to be a real gem. The grid-based combat system reminds me of classics like Final Fantasy Tactics, but with its own unique mechanics. The synergy between different hero abilities creates interesting strategic options - for example, using the ice mage to freeze enemies in place and then having your warrior use area attacks for maximum damage. I appreciate that the game is quite generous with resources for free players, though progress does slow down in the later stages. The gear system adds another layer of strategy, especially with the set bonuses that can completely change how a character functions. My advice for mid-game players is to focus on fully upgrading a core team of 6-8 heroes rather than spreading your resources too thin across many characters. The Dragon Trials mode that unlocks at level 30 offers some of the best rewards in the game but requires carefully tailored teams for each challenge. My only criticism would be that some of the later story missions feel a bit grindy, requiring multiple attempts even with well-equipped heroes. Overall though, it's one of the most engaging browser RPGs I've played in years!
