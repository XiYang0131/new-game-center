About Screw Out Bolts and Nuts

Screw Out Bolts and Nuts is a relaxing and satisfying puzzle game where you need to unscrew bolts from various mechanical contraptions. Test your problem-solving skills as you figure out the correct order and method to remove each bolt. With increasingly complex mechanisms and challenges, this game offers a perfect blend of casual gameplay and brain-teasing puzzles.

Features:

  • Dozens of unique levels with increasing difficulty
  • Realistic physics and satisfying unscrewing mechanics
  • Various types of bolts, nuts, and mechanical parts to interact with
  • Relaxing gameplay with no time pressure
  • Intuitive controls that are easy to learn
  • Calming background music and satisfying sound effects

How to Play:

Click and hold on a bolt, then rotate your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to unscrew it. Some bolts may require you to remove other parts first before they can be accessed. Pay attention to the structure of each mechanism to determine the correct order of removal. The goal is to remove all bolts and disassemble the contraption completely. If you get stuck, try looking for bolts that aren't blocked by other parts.
