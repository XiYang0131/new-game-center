About Screw Out Bolts and Nuts

Screw Out Bolts and Nuts is an engaging physics-based puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills. In this game, you need to carefully unscrew bolts and nuts from various contraptions without letting them fall into the wrong places. Each level presents a unique challenge with increasing complexity, requiring you to think strategically about the order and direction in which you remove each piece.

Features

  • Dozens of challenging levels with progressive difficulty
  • Realistic physics simulation for authentic unscrewing mechanics
  • Various types of bolts, nuts, and contraptions to solve
  • Intuitive touch and mouse controls
  • Time-based challenges for additional replay value
  • Relaxing gameplay with no time pressure on regular levels
  • Satisfying visual and sound effects when successfully completing a level

How to Play

Use your mouse to interact with the bolts and nuts. Click and hold on a bolt or nut, then rotate your mouse in the appropriate direction to unscrew it. Pay close attention to the threading direction - some bolts require clockwise rotation while others need counter-clockwise rotation to loosen. Be careful not to let pieces fall into restricted areas or the level will fail. Plan your approach carefully, as the order in which you remove pieces can significantly impact the difficulty. Some levels have special mechanics like gravity shifts or moving platforms that add extra challenge. Take your time to analyze each puzzle before making your first move!
