About Twisted Tangle NMT

Twisted Tangle NMT is a captivating puzzle game that challenges your spatial reasoning and problem-solving skills. The concept is elegantly simple yet deeply engaging: untangle a web of intersecting lines by moving nodes around until no lines cross each other.

What makes this game special is its perfect balance of accessibility and challenge. The early levels ease you into the mechanics, while later puzzles will test even the most experienced puzzle enthusiasts. The colorful visual design not only looks appealing but also helps you identify which nodes are connected, making the game intuitive to play.

As you progress through the levels, you'll encounter increasingly complex tangles with more nodes and connections. The satisfaction of transforming a chaotic jumble of lines into a clean, organized pattern is immensely rewarding. With hundreds of handcrafted puzzles to solve, Twisted Tangle NMT offers hours of brain-teasing entertainment.

Features

  • Over 200 challenging puzzles with increasing difficulty
  • Intuitive drag-and-drop controls
  • Colorful visual design that aids gameplay
  • Hint system for when you get stuck
  • Level progression that gradually introduces new concepts
  • Relaxing background music that enhances focus
  • No time limits - solve puzzles at your own pace
