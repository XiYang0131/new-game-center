About Knock and Run

Knock and Run is an exciting action game that tests your speed, reflexes, and timing. In this game, you play as a mischievous character who knocks on doors and must run away before getting caught. Navigate through different neighborhoods, avoid obstacles, and escape angry residents as you try to knock on as many doors as possible!

Features

  • Fast-paced action gameplay
  • Multiple challenging levels
  • Various obstacles and enemies
  • Power-ups to help your escape
  • Increasing difficulty as you progress

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character. Press the spacebar to knock on doors. Time your knocks carefully and plan your escape route to avoid getting caught. Collect power-ups to run faster or temporarily stun pursuers. Complete each level by knocking on all the required doors without getting caught!
