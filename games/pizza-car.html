About Pizza Car

Pizza Car is a fun driving simulation game where you take on the role of a pizza delivery driver. Navigate through busy city streets, avoid obstacles, and deliver pizzas to hungry customers before time runs out. Earn money from successful deliveries to upgrade your car and become the best pizza delivery driver in town!

Features:

  • Realistic driving physics with multiple vehicle options
  • Detailed city environment with various delivery locations
  • Time-based delivery challenges with increasing difficulty
  • Car upgrade system to improve speed, handling, and durability
  • Day and night cycle with changing traffic conditions
  • Achievement system to track your progress
  • Fun and addictive gameplay suitable for all ages

How to Play:

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car. Press the up arrow or W to accelerate, down arrow or S to brake/reverse, and left/right arrows or A/D to steer. Follow the navigation arrow to find your delivery destination. Deliver pizzas within the time limit to earn money. Be careful not to damage your car by crashing into obstacles or other vehicles. Use earned money to upgrade your car's performance between deliveries. Complete as many deliveries as possible to become the ultimate pizza delivery driver!
