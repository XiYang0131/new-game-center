About Dig Craft

Dig Craft is an exciting sandbox adventure game inspired by Minecraft. In this game, you can mine various resources, craft tools and items, and build amazing structures. Explore a vast procedurally generated world, discover caves, fight monsters, and let your creativity run wild as you shape the environment to your liking.

Features

  • Open-world sandbox gameplay
  • Mine various resources like wood, stone, and minerals
  • Craft tools, weapons, and building materials
  • Build structures and shape the environment
  • Day and night cycle with different challenges
  • Encounter various creatures and monsters

How to Play

Use WASD keys to move your character. Left-click to break blocks and right-click to place blocks. Press E to open your inventory and crafting menu. Collect resources by breaking blocks, then use the crafting menu to create tools and building materials. Start by collecting wood, then craft a workbench to access more advanced crafting recipes. Build shelters to protect yourself during the night when monsters appear!
